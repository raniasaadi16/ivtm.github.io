What's about event

The International Virtual Travel Market (IVTM) is an event that will welcome professionals in the travel industry.

The online event will connect professionals worldwide through the use of virtual booths, 1:1 meetings, group networking, webinars, and workshops.

Our event is designed to be 100% virtual. This will allow the likes of attendees, speakers, exhibitors, and sponsors to easily participate and discuss their ideas.

The IVTM will occur twice yearly - one in March and one in September.

Who should take part?

The IVTM will welcome all types of professionals within the travel industry.

This can include anyone related to DMCs, Travel Agencies, Tour Operators, Accommodations, Hospitality Technology Companies, Digital Marketing Companies, and many more.

2 You will save valuable time and effort

With the IVTM, you will not need to worry about unnecessary documentation, organising travelling efforts, how strong your networking ability is, or waste time waiting around for other people.

In this virtual event, all you will need is your laptop, an internet connection, and a working webcam.

3 Allowance of more representatives

In a physical event, you will only be able to send one or two of your representatives to network. With the IVTM, however, you will have the luxury of allowing up to 10 representatives within your virtual team. As well as this, every user will have the flexibility to set up their own personal meeting availability times.

4 The ability to know your visitors

With physical events, you can often get visitors that might not know of who you are, let alone be interested in working with you.

In the IVTM on the other hand, there is a feature that ensures any visitors will have to pre-request a meeting with you by choosing a free time slot of their choice.

When a user requests a meeting with you, you will be able to read their profile, letting you see their topics of interests and discussions before you can choose to accept or reject their request.

This will save a lot of wasted networking sessions.

5 The ability to search and match

Just like any social networking site, the IVTM will allow users to search and match with potential customers or visitors.

For example, if you are working for a Hotel, you have the ability to search for travel agencies, and then you can discuss business directly.

All you need to do is search the visitors list and start a chat with them, allowing you to achieve exhibition goals much easier.

6 Easy sharing

This digital event makes the invitation process much easier.

It can sometimes take potential clients a lot of thought on whether they should attend a physical event, but with the IVTM, you can easily share a link to your virtual booth by email, social media, or any other digital means.
