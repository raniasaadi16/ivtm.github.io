About IVTM

The International Virtual Travel Market (IVTM) is an event that will welcome professionals in the travel industry.

The online event will connect professionals worldwide through the use of virtual booths, 1:1 meetings, group networking, webinars, and workshops.

Our event is designed to be 100% virtual. This will allow the likes of attendees, speakers, exhibitors, and sponsors to easily participate and discuss their ideas.

The IVTM will occur twice yearly - one in March and one in September.

Who should take part?

The IVTM will welcome all types of professionals within the travel industry.

This can include anyone related to DMCs, Travel Agencies, Tour Operators, Accommodations, Hospitality Technology Companies, Digital Marketing Companies, and many more.

The IVTM will be a place for seeking insights and valuable connections within the global travel industry. We will be bringing together a variety of professionals in order to create endless opportunities for you and your associates. When taking part as a visitor, you will benefit from networking, webinars, seminars, and the opportunity to speak directly with potential stakeholders.

Here are some reasons why visiting the IVTM will be an invaluable experience.

1

As this is a virtual event, there is no need to worry about travel expenses or entry fees. Attending the IVTM is completely free.

2

The IVTM allows visitors to search for exactly what you need in just one click. Unlike the busy floorplans and hectic atmosphere of a physical event, you will easily be able to search for exhibitors that are a matching fit for your needs.

3

Digital exhibition data. Have you ever attended a physical event and been overwhelmed with the amount of business cards and brochures that you received? With the IVTM, all this information will be available for you to save digitally to email, allowing you to access this at any time in the future.

4

In the IVTM, you can enjoy the luxury of free webinars with professionals in your industry. These are easy to organise, intimate, and effective compared to most physical meetings.

5

With the addition of the digital networking table, you are free to join in with the discussion at any time; allowing you to discuss many ideas with like-minded people.
